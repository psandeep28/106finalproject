Understanding Student Depression Risks

Sleep Duration & Depression Risk

Students who sleep fewer than 6 hours per night are more than twice as likely to experience depression compared to those who sleep 7–8 hours.

Depression Rate by Sleep Duration:

  • Less than 6 hours: 63%
  • 7–8 hours: 28%

Work/Study Hours & Depression Risk

Students who reported experiencing depression spend over 1.5 hours more per day working or studying compared to their non-depressed peers.

Average Daily Work/Study Time:

  • With Depression: 7.81 hours/day
  • Without Depression: 6.24 hours/day
